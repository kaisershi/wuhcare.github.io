Privacy Policy for WuhCare Plus

This Privacy Policy explains how WuhCare Plus collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the mobile application. WuhCare Plus is a healthcare service application for hospital-related services such as authentication, appointments, notifications, laboratory results, vaccine history, health checkup information, clinic information, and patient profile access.

Effective date: April 21, 2026
App name: WuhCare Plus
Android package: com.wuhithospital.wuhcareplus

1. Information We Collect

Depending on the features you use, the app may collect or process the following categories of information:

  • Account credentials such as username and password used to sign in.
  • Authentication data such as access tokens, refresh tokens, session data, PIN, and login timestamps.
  • Patient identifiers such as hospital number (HN) and profile information returned by the hospital system.
  • Health and medical information made available to you by the hospital, including appointments, laboratory results, vaccine records, drug allergy information, and health checkup reports.
  • Push notification token and notification preferences used to deliver reminders and alerts.
  • Technical and usage information such as app events, screen access history, API request logs, device platform, and error logs for debugging and service improvement.

2. How We Use Information

  • To authenticate you and keep your session active.
  • To retrieve and display your hospital-related data from authorized backend systems.
  • To send appointment reminders, medication reminders, lab result alerts, and other app notifications.
  • To improve app stability, diagnose failures, and maintain service security.
  • To support optional device security features such as PIN and biometric-based access control.

3. Permissions the App May Request

  • Camera: to support scanning or image-based features where applicable.
  • Microphone: may be requested by media-related libraries when audio-enabled capture is used.
  • Notifications: to send healthcare-related reminders and alerts.
  • Biometric or fingerprint access: to support secure device-level authentication where enabled.
  • Media or file access: to display, download, open, or share documents such as reports and PDF files.

You can manage most permissions in your device settings at any time.

4. Storage and Security

Some information is stored locally on your device to keep you signed in, remember preferences, support PIN access, cache app data, and improve performance. Data is transmitted to backend services over HTTPS.

We take reasonable measures to protect information handled by the app. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data.

Information may be processed or shared only as needed with:

  • Hospital backend systems and authentication infrastructure required to provide services.
  • Push notification service providers used to deliver app notifications.
  • Service components involved in app hosting, security, and technical operations, only where necessary to operate the service.

6. Health Information

Because WuhCare Plus is a healthcare application, the app may display and process sensitive health-related information provided by hospital systems, including appointment information, laboratory results, immunization history, health checkup reports, and related patient profile data.

This information is used only to provide healthcare-related services inside the app and is handled in connection with the hospital services you access.

7. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to operate the app, maintain login state, support medical service access, comply with hospital operational requirements, and resolve security or technical issues. Some locally stored logs may be kept temporarily for debugging.

8. Your Choices

  • You may log out of the app to clear active session data.
  • You may disable notifications through your device settings.
  • You may remove or reset PIN access inside the app where available.
  • You may contact the service provider if you need help regarding your data or account.

9. Children's Privacy

This app is intended for authorized patients and users of hospital services. It is not designed as a general audience app for children acting independently. Where an account relates to a minor patient, use of the app should be managed by an authorized parent, guardian, or lawful representative as applicable.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be reflected on the page where this policy is published, together with a revised effective date.
